Stephen Gary Wozniak was born on August 11, 1950, in San Jose, California.[4]: 18 [11][12]: 13 [13]: 27  His mother, Margaret Louise Wozniak (née Kern) (1923–2014),[14] was from Washington state, and his father, Francis Jacob "Jerry" Wozniak (1925–1994) of Michigan,[4]: 18  was an engineer for the Lockheed Corporation.[13]: 1  Wozniak graduated from Homestead High School in 1968, in Cupertino, California.[12]: 25  The name on Wozniak's birth certificate is "Stephan Gary Wozniak", but his mother said that she intended it to be spelled "Stephen", which is what he uses.[4]: 18  Wozniak has mentioned his surname being Polish.[15][4]: 129–130 [N 1] In the early 1970s, Wozniak's blue box design earned him the nickname "Berkeley Blue" in the phreaking community.[1][17] Wozniak has credited watching Star Trek and attending Star Trek conventions while in his youth as a source of inspiration for his starting Apple Inc.[18]
